How to read this map
Summary of key layers and how they connect to KID Museum’s outreach strategy.
  • Dots: MCPS + DC schools (with program participation where applicable).
  • Colors: General Admissions, After-School, and Field Trip visits.
  • Buffers: 1-mile rings around schools flagged as Title I, CEP, high FARMs, underserved, or underrepresented.
  • Rings: 10-mile and 25-mile catchment areas around Ft Totten and Bethesda Metro KID Museum sites.
